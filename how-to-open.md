# How to Open Lintel (Beta)

Because Lintel is distributed outside the Mac App Store, macOS will warn
you that it's from an "unidentified developer." This is expected for beta.

1. Download Lintel.zip and unzip it.
2. **Do NOT double-click** the app the first time.
3. **Right-click** (or Control-click) Lintel.app → choose **Open**.
4. A dialog appears: click the **Open** button.
5. Lintel will launch and appear in your menu bar.

Future updates will be delivered automatically via the built-in updater.
